Exploring Dublin: A City of History and Culture

May 7, 2024

I had the pleasure of diving into Dublin's rich history and vibrant culture, beginning my tour at the expansive and beautiful Phoenix Park, followed by a visit to the historic old barracks used in various wars throughout Dublin's history. The solemn Arbour Hill Cemetery and Memorial, where people of Irish independence are commemorated, provided a moment of deep reflection for all of us in the class.

The day concluded with a walk through Dublin's streets, where I soaked in the unique atmosphere and vibrant city life. I capped off my exploration with a quick dinner and a visit to a couple of Irish pubs which were reccommended to me. I truly enjoyed the traditional music and warm hospitality that I experienced during my first day in Dublin.

Guinness Storehouse Tour

May 8, 2024

The Guinness Storehouse was a very cool experience. It was super interesting to learn about all the intricate details and the science behind making a pint of Guinness. The Guinness Storehouse felt more like a building out of Willy Wonka than a regular old factory. I had a blast!
