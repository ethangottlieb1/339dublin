The Cliffs

The Cliffs of Moher were truly beautiful. I had an amazing time walking along the cliffs and looking out at the Atlantic Ocean. We got very lucky with the beautiful weather so it was a perfect day to see the everlasting view. Truly an incredible experience and I would love to see them again if I visit Ireland in the future.
